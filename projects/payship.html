Project Details

One Interface for All Your Shipping

A unified hub that connects users and merchants with multiple shipping companies, compares prices and SLAs, and provides end-to-end tracking and notifications.

The Problem

Shipping a parcel sounds simple‚Äîuntil you actually try to do it. Without Payship, users and merchants often face:

  • Fragmented experience: Every carrier has a different website, flow, and terminology.
  • Unclear pricing: Hidden fees, changing rates, and no easy way to compare options side-by-side.
  • Poor visibility: Tracking links scattered across emails; no single place to see all shipments and their statuses.
  • Time sink for merchants: Creating dozens of shipments one by one across multiple portals.
  • Inconsistent SLAs: Delivery times and pickup windows vary, making planning difficult.

The result is confusion, delays, and higher costs‚Äîespecially when shipping volume grows or when shipments have different destinations and constraints.

The Solution

Payship consolidates major shipping companies into a single, consistent interface. Enter your shipment details once, and Payship:

  1. Normalizes your data across carriers.
  2. Surfaces the best options ranked by price, ETA, and reliability.
  3. Creates and manages your labels and pickups.
  4. Keeps you informed with real-time status updates and notifications.

How It Works ?

For Individuals

  1. Enter origin and destination.
  2. Provide parcel details (weight, dimensions, contents).
  3. Review matched carriers with instant price and ETA comparison.
  4. Select the best option, confirm, and pay.
  5. Track your shipment from one dashboard‚Äîno more juggling links.

For Merchants

  1. Download the Excel template.
  2. Fill in multiple orders (destinations, parcel info, service preferences).
  3. Upload the file and let Payship auto-create all shipments.
  4. Bulk-select the preferred carrier per order or apply global rules.
  5. Confirm, pay, and monitor all shipments in a single view.

Key Benefits

  • Clarity over chaos: One place to compare prices, ETAs, and services across carriers.
  • Real savings: Optimized recommendations help reduce shipping costs.
  • Time back: Bulk creation via Excel for high-volume merchants.
  • Full visibility: Unified tracking and status notifications for every shipment.
  • Scalable workflows: Works for a single parcel or thousands of orders.

Before vs. After Payship

Before

  • Multiple tabs, multiple logins, inconsistent forms.
  • Manual price checks and guesswork on delivery times.
  • Scattered tracking links and missed status changes.
  • Re-entering the same data for each carrier.

After

  • Single login, unified flow, consistent data entry.
  • Transparent side-by-side comparison of price and ETA.
  • One dashboard for tracking and notifications.
  • Bulk creation and carrier selection rules for merchants.

Core Features

  • Multi-carrier integration (domestic and international).
  • Real-time rates and service availability.
  • Unified tracking & push/email notifications.
  • Excel bulk import for high-volume orders.
  • Label generation and pickup scheduling.
  • Audit trail and shipment history.

Outcomes

  • Reduced time-to-ship for merchants handling large batches.
  • Lower total shipping cost via smart recommendations.
  • Fewer support tickets thanks to clear, unified tracking.
