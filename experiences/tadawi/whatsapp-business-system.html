Overview

Omni-Inbox & Campaign Manager (WhatsApp Business Integration)

A centralized platform to manage social messaging, automate customer journeys, run campaigns, and hand off conversations to human agentsâ€”starting with a real-time WhatsApp Business integration.

The Problem

Businesses that rely on WhatsApp to talk to customers often struggle with scattered chats across devices, no clear ownership, limited automation, and weak visibility into performance. Managers cannot easily route conversations, measure team productivity, or design guided flows that reduce handling time. Media shared over WhatsApp also becomes unavailable after a limited period, which affects history and context.

The Solution

This system brings all customer messaging into one place and adds an automation layer on top of WhatsApp Business:

  • Real-time ingestion: Receive messages instantly from the company WhatsApp number and display them in the agent console.
  • Configurable bot: A template-driven bot that guides users with structured options (talk to agent, booking inquiry, service info, doctor appointment, and more).
  • Smart routing: Auto-assign chats to the right Team (not just a generic department) for faster, more accurate handling.
  • Human handoff: Seamlessly transfer conversations to Customer Care when needed, with full audit logging.
  • Template management: Create, sync, and send WhatsApp message templates at scale with easy variable handling.
  • Retention and visibility: Persist attachments on our servers so agents always see historical files, even after external links expire.

Key Capabilities

  • Omni-Inbox: Unified view of all WhatsApp conversations with statuses, assignees, and timestamps.
  • Teams & Roles: Create Teams (e.g., Bookings, Support, Sales) and assign agents; route conversations directly to the relevant team.
  • Bot Builder: Define template messages with buttons/options; chain messages into flows; attach events (e.g., create booking) to user choices.
  • Agent Handoff: Auto-escalate to a human agent when rules match (intent, keywords, user choice, or failure cases).
  • Audit Trail: Track who took the chat, when it was claimed, every message sender, and status changes.
  • Customer Feedback: After a conversation is closed, send an automatic CSAT survey to rate the experience.
  • Template Management: Build templates in the app, publish via WhatsApp Business API, and sync existing approved templates.
  • Variables Support: Simple, non-technical way to inject dynamic fields (name, booking ID, date, etc.).
  • Attachments Storage: Store media on our servers to keep history available and visible for agents.
  • Broadcasts & Campaigns: Send templates to large audiences; collect interaction metrics per user.
  • Analytics: Monitor volume, first response time, resolution time, CSAT, agent productivity, and campaign performance.

Conversation Flow

  1. Inbound: A customer messages the company WhatsApp number.
  2. Bot Greeting: The system replies with a template containing options (e.g., talk to agent, booking inquiry, service info, doctor appointment).
  3. User Choice: The user taps a button or sends a keyword to continue.
  4. Automation: Based on the path, the bot can:
    • Answer FAQs or provide service details.
    • Create a booking or collect required data.
    • Route the chat to a specific Team for human follow-up.
  5. Handoff: An agent in the target Team claims the conversation and chats in real-time.
  6. Closure & CSAT: When the conversation ends (by agent or user), the system sends a rating request automatically.

From Template Bot to LLM-First Customer Service

Build an intelligent, secure, and human-centred platform where large language models (LLMs) handle most conversations end-to-end, and seamlessly hand off to agents when human judgment is required.

Why Now

Template bots are rigid, hard to scale. An LLM-first approach understands intent, asks clarifying questions, executes tasks, and learns from outcomesâ€”delivering faster, more personal, and more consistent support.

Strategic Pillars

  • LLM-First Assistant: Natural, multilingual, context-aware conversations that adapt to user intentâ€”not rigid menus.
  • Human-in-the-Loop: Instant, graceful handoff to agents with full context; agents can coach and correct the model.
  • Tool Use & Automation: The assistant can call internal tools (create bookings, check orders, schedule appointments) via safe, auditable functions.
  • Trust, Safety, and Compliance: Guardrails, PII redaction, role-based access, audit trails, and opt-in data retention.
  • Unified Knowledge: Retrieval-augmented generation over product, policy, and historical chats for accurate, grounded answers.
  • Insights & Learning: Continuous quality metrics, feedback loops, and automatic improvement of templates and prompts.
  • Extensibility: Channel-agnostic (WhatsApp first, then Instagram, Messenger, web chat), modular skills, and APIs.

LLM Evolution Roadmap

  1. Phase 1 â€” Retrieval-Augmented FAQ: Grounded answers from internal knowledge base and prior conversations. Agent review for low-confidence replies.
  2. Phase 2 â€” Task Execution via Tools: The assistant calls safe functions (create booking, lookup order, cancel, reschedule) with parameter validation and audit logs.
  3. Phase 3 â€” Smart Triage & Routing: Automatic classification by intent, priority, sentiment, and customer value; route to the right Team or keep automated.
  4. Phase 4 â€” Proactive Coaching & Drafting: The model drafts agent replies, suggests next actions, and highlights risk or compliance flags before sending.
  5. Phase 5 â€” Autonomous Campaigns (Guardrailed): Generate, A/B test, and personalize WhatsApp templates; throttle and safety checks to avoid spam and policy violations.

Safety & Guardrails

  • PII detection and redaction in logs; configurable retention windows.
  • Function calling allow-list with strict schemas, dry-runs, and approvals for risky actions.
  • Confidence thresholds with automatic escalation to human agents.
  • Per-team permissions, audit trails, and export controls.
